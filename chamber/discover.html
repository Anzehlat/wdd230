Supporting the Northern Chambers

The Pleasant Grove Chamber of Commerce participates in many combined activities and events with our neighbor chambers, Point of the Mountain Chamber, Eagle Mountain Chamber and the American Fork Chamber of Commerce. Each Chamber is dedicated to it's own members, but also sees the great value in expanding our member networking opportunities. Look through the Events and Programs tab and join us in an even more expansive marketplace for your business.
